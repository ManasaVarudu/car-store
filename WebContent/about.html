About US

Our customers can search for, find, buy and even finance their next used car online, from the comfort of their own home. What’s more, hundreds of our cars can be delivered to a store near you


If you'd prefer to visit our lovely associates members, we'll welcome you into our friendly and relaxed stores that are designed around making the car buying experience simple and enjoyable - which is how it should be!

About our cars

For your total peace of mind, every car we sell is thoroughly inspected by a fully trained Technician and comes with a three month guarantee, with the option to extend this for a further two years. All of our cars under a 104-point mechanical and cosmetic inspection to ensure that they are sold to you in tip-top condition. We also guarantee that the cars we sell are the lowest priced in the market, if you find the same car at a local competitor cheaper - we'll refund you the difference.

We get it, buying a car is a big deal. So, to ensure that you're head over heels about your new car and give you that added peace of mind when buying, we offer a 7 Day Money Back Guarantee. This means if there's something not quite right, you can return it or exchange for another car from us.

With thousands of quality cars to choose from and the option to part exchange, once you have bought your dream car, we'll then look after you with tailored aftercare packages.

Just looking to sell? That's okay too - we'll buy your car even if you don't buy another one from us.
